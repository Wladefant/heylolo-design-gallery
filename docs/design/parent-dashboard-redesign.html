Parent Dashboard — redesign

A calmer, flatter take on the parent home (P1). Same warm teal tide-pool brand and the same content the real screen carries — child picker, weekly mood, current companion, today's usage, today's insight and recent prompts — but hierarchy now comes from type and whitespace instead of nested cards and chevrons everywhere. Three live states shown side by side. Monthly Review and Perspective Alignment are intentionally out of scope.

Tap the child pill to open the switcher (state 3). Hover a prompt to see the "Ask" affordance. Numerals + stats set in DM Sans; everything else Nunito.
