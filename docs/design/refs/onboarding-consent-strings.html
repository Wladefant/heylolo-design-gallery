2. COPPA parental-consent screen

Fires at child-profile creation (app-review-submission-dossier.md §1 [5.1.1], §2 line 4) — a separate, earlier gate than §3 (general data consent; §3 is the AI-specific disclosure right before first message). Legal lines use the impersonal carve-out from glossary §1; checkbox/CTA stay Du.

Element EN DE
Header Before {name} can start Bevor {name} loslegen kann
What-we-collect summary (honest, short — 3 lines, matches FAQ Q5/Q10) Here's what we collect and why: {name}'s name, age, and conversations with LoLo — stored in the EU, used only to make LoLo work and to keep {name} safe. We never sell it, and you can delete it anytime. Das erheben wir, und warum: Name, Alter und die Gespräche von {name} mit LoLo — gespeichert in der EU, nur genutzt, damit LoLo funktioniert und {name} sicher ist. Wir verkaufen nichts davon, und du kannst es jederzeit löschen.
Consent checkbox line I confirm I am the parent or legal guardian of this child, and I agree to the collection described above. Ich bestätige, dass ich Elternteil oder gesetzlich erziehungsberechtigte Person dieses Kindes bin, und stimme der oben beschriebenen Erhebung zu.
Link label — privacy policy Read our full Privacy Policy Vollständige Datenschutzerklärung lesen
Link label — data rights Your rights over this data Deine Rechte zu diesen Daten
Primary button I agree — continue Ich stimme zu — weiter
Secondary (declines the app for this child) Not right now Jetzt nicht

Deliberately narrower than the full Privacy Policy — the "honest, short" summary the brief asks for, checked against FAQ Q5/Q10. Does not name Gemini/Vertex — that's the standalone §3 card, kept separate per Apple's "not bundled" rule.

3. THE 5.1.2(i) card

Per app-review-submission-dossier.md §1/§4#1/§6: must be standalone (not bundled with mic/notification prompts), name the provider in plain text, fire before the first message ever sends, single explicit consent action. Fires once, after §2, right before the disc's first turn. Data line must match faq-help-center-copy.md Q5: EU storage, Gemini processing currently partly in the US (never claim all-EU — gated on #177).

Variant A — Formal-minimal

Element EN DE
Heading How LoLo's AI works Wie LoLos KI funktioniert
Body LoLo's replies are generated by Google Gemini, an AI system run by Google. To create a reply, {name}'s message is sent to Google's Vertex AI, processed in the EU and partly in the US, then deleted from Google's side. We never use it to train Google's models. LoLos Antworten werden von Google Gemini erzeugt, einem KI-System von Google. Um eine Antwort zu erstellen, wird die Nachricht von {name} an Googles Vertex AI gesendet, in der EU und teilweise in den USA verarbeitet und danach auf Googles Seite gelöscht. Wir nutzen sie nie zum Training der Modelle von Google.
Consent button (single action) Agree and continue Zustimmen und weiter
Decline link Not now Jetzt nicht

Variant B — Warm-but-explicit (recommended, see below)

Element EN DE
Heading LoLo needs Google's help to talk LoLo braucht Googles Hilfe zum Sprechen
Body Every time {name} talks to LoLo, the message travels to Google Gemini — the AI that writes LoLo's replies — to be turned into an answer. Your child's data is stored in the EU; Google currently processes the message itself partly in the US (we're working to bring all of it to the EU). Google never uses it to train their models. (Retention: verify our Vertex zero-retention configuration before shipping this sentence — do not claim instant deletion unless configured; see #177/DPA review.) Immer wenn {name} mit LoLo spricht, reist die Nachricht zu Google Gemini — der KI, die LoLos Antworten schreibt — um daraus eine Antwort zu machen. Die Daten deines Kindes werden in der EU gespeichert; Google verarbeitet die Nachricht selbst derzeit teilweise in den USA (wir arbeiten daran, das vollständig in die EU zu holen). Google nutzt sie nie zum Training eigener Modelle. (Aufbewahrung: vor dem Versand die Vertex-Zero-Retention-Konfiguration prüfen — keine Sofortlöschung behaupten, solange nicht konfiguriert; siehe #177/DPA-Prüfung.)
Consent button (single action) Yes, let LoLo talk with {name} Ja, lass LoLo mit {name} sprechen
Decline link Not right now Jetzt nicht

Decline path (both variants): Declining does not delete the profile or exit onboarding — it returns to the handoff card with chat locked. No text-only fallback (Gemini generates every reply in both modes). Locked-state banner:

Element EN DE
Locked-state banner (post-decline) Turn on AI chat in Settings whenever you're ready — {name} can't talk to LoLo until then. Schalte den KI-Chat in den Einstellungen ein, sobald du bereit bist — bis dahin kann {name} nicht mit LoLo sprechen.
Settings re-surface — row label AI processing KI-Verarbeitung
Settings re-surface — row subtitle Review or turn off how LoLo's replies are generated. Überprüfe oder schalte aus, wie LoLos Antworten erzeugt werden.

Recommendation: ship Variant B (warm-but-explicit)

Reasoning: Variant A is legally sufficient but reads like a permissions dialog a parent thumbs past — the "rubber-stamp consent" pattern Apple's Nov-13-2025 update is hunting (dossier §4 #1, "highest-probability, newest rule"). Variant B keeps every fact identical (Gemini named plainly, EU+partial-US line, no-training promise, single action) but reads as LoLo's own voice — matching onboarding's warm register (§1) and FAQ Q4/Q5's "full visibility, not a black box" line. A reviewer is more likely to read B fully than skim A. Both satisfy 5.1.2(i) on the letter; B on the spirit — safer for "the ONE thing to fix before Aug 10" (dossier §6).

4. Trial start screen

Position: after the kid's first wonder, never before (onboarding-paywall-teardowns.md §B1/B2, invest-then-ask). Copy is the settled §B2 canon, reconciled with glossary §4 (#13, #20) and FAQ Q9.

Element EN DE
Header 14 days free. No card. No catch. 14 Tage kostenlos. Ohne Kreditkarte. Ohne Haken.
Body LoLo is all yours for two weeks — every story, every wonder, every voice chat. We won't ask for a card until you decide to stay. LoLo gehört euch zwei Wochen lang ganz allein — jede Geschichte, jedes Wunder, jeder Sprachchat. Wir fragen erst nach einer Karte, wenn du dich entscheidest zu bleiben.
Voice-cap framing line (new — ties the trial to the existing Screen Time/Quiet Hours cap so "unlimited" isn't overstated) Voice chats count toward your child's daily time, same as everything else — set or change the limit anytime in Settings. Sprachchats zählen zur täglichen Zeit deines Kindes, genau wie alles andere — das Limit kannst du jederzeit in den Einstellungen anpassen.
Primary button Start my 14 free days Meine 14 kostenlosen Tage starten
Secondary button (quiet) Not now — keep exploring Jetzt nicht — weiter entdecken
Fine print On day 14 we'll email you first. Keep it for $59.99/year (≈$5/mo) or do nothing and it simply ends. Cancel in two taps, anytime. Am 14. Tag schreiben wir dir zuerst. Behalte LoLo für 59,99 $/Jahr (≈5 $/Monat), oder tu einfach nichts, dann endet die Testphase von selbst. Kündigen in zwei Tipps, jederzeit.

Why the line exists: "every voice chat" could read as literally unlimited. The app has a daily Screen Time cap (FAQ Q3 / glossary "Zeitlimit") regardless of subscription state — this pre-empts "wait, why did it stop?" by naming the real mechanism up front.

5. First-session opener support strings (#179)

Per day0-activation-playbook.md §1 Phase C: the closed binary ice-breaker must never leave a kid facing a dead mic. {optionA}/{optionB} come from the per-interest content set, not translated here.

Element EN DE
Tap-chip fallback header (shown above the two option buttons alongside the spoken question, available from turn one — not only after a mic failure) Or tap one: Oder tipp einfach:
Tap-chip buttons {optionA} / {optionB} (verbatim, e.g. "T-Rex" / "Triceratops") {optionA} / {optionB} (unverändert übernommen)
Dead-mic re-offer line (spoken by LoLo after ~6s of silence — one gentle re-prompt, per the playbook's "never leave an open mic into silence") You can just say {optionA} or {optionB}! Du kannst einfach {optionA} oder {optionB} sagen!
Fallback micro-nudge (appears with the re-offer, pointing at the chips already on screen) Or just tap one below. Oder tipp einfach unten drauf.

Design note: chips are on-screen from the first instant, not summoned only after a mic failure — the re-offer line just nudges toward chips already there ("one gentle re-prompt, then a tap fallback," §1 Phase C).
