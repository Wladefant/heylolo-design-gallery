Disc-as-anchor, calm then alive

The pale-teal mic disc holds its position in every state — only its energy changes. At rest the white rings breathe on opacity; on tap it springs to scale 1.07, VU bars pulse, ripples bloom and the grid recedes. Pattern from Duolingo ABC (pip-in-halo) + Meta AI (the single voice orb that wakes).

Soft tinted tiles, not heavy cards

Four mode tiles on white with a whisper-tinted icon blob each — teal for explore, a warm hint for stories. Calm, rounded, low-contrast borders rather than boxed-in cards. Tile feel from Wysa (soft 2×2) and Grok voice (leading-icon mode row).

One dock, two depths

Resting home shows just the disc + "or type." The richer state fans the dock to + · disc · Explorer so talk, attach, and live-explore live side by side — without ever burying the disc. "Tap to speak" label clarity borrowed from Duolingo.
